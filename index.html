    About

    I love to help others and I offer a blend of relaxation and treatment focus massage that leaves my clients feeling whole again.

    If I can help make my client's life easier and more enjoyable by increasing their abitily to perform their daily activites, I feel like I've accomplished my goal.

    I graduated from Brenneke School of Massage in 2005 and have taken a number of continuing education courses to round out the services I can provide. My toolset includes Swedish, Deep Tissue, Myofasical Release, Lymphatic Drainage, Pre-natal massage and I have experience treating stress/anxiety, tension and posture issues from Computer Work, Low Back Pain

    When I'm not caring for my clients, I'm enjoying the outdoors, sewing or cooking with my family.
